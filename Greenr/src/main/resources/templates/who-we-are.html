Who We Are

We are a group of students studying at Brunel University who have taken on the task of creating a website that provides a tool for communities to organize community clean ups as well as an accurate Air Quality Index to determine pollution within an area.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a post?

Simply upload a photo to the website using the 'Community' tab by choosing the 'create a post' option.

What is AQI?

AQI is an acronym for Air Quality Index, this is used to represent the quality of the air from the levels of pollutants present- the lower the better!

I've made a post, now what?

Now it's time to wait for other users to respond to your post, we will notify you when they do!
